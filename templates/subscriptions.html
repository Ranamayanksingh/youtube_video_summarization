How it works

  1. Message @userinfobot on Telegram to get your Chat ID.

  3. Add a subscription above with a YouTube channel and your preferred daily time.
  4. Every day at that time, the latest video from the channel is downloaded, transcribed, and summarised. The 5 key highlights arrive in your Telegram — before you even make coffee.
  5. Click Send test to verify the connection right now.
